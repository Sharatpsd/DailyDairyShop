Our Story

Where every drop begins

We began with a single, simple belief — that every family in Bangladesh deserves dairy that is truly pure. Not just labeled as such, but verifiably, honestly, undeniably fresh. From ethically raised cows to carefully temperature-controlled delivery, every step is done with intention.

🐄
Ethical Farming

Our cows are raised on open pasture, never given artificial hormones or antibiotics.

🌿
Zero Preservatives

No chemicals, no additives. What comes from the farm, goes straight to your home.

🌅
Morning Delivery

Milk is collected at dawn and delivered fresh every single morning, seven days a week.

🔬
Quality Tested

Every batch passes rigorous freshness and purity checks before leaving our facility.

Our Journey

Four years of dedication

2021

2024
Introduced seamless online ordering and home delivery — fresh dairy at your fingertips.
